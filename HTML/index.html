    About Our Journey

    Welcome to Perfect Bible Study

    Perfect Bible Study was born out of a passion to bring clarity to the life and teachings of Jesus through the 39 parables He Himself used. Our mission is to help people understand the Word of God in its purest form — simple, direct, and full of truth. With every video, we aim to light a path that brings hearts closer to God's Word. Read More

    Glorify God

    Everything we do is to lift up the name of Jesus and reflect His light in today’s world.

    Believe Bible

    We trust the Bible as the unchanging truth and base every teaching on Jesus' parables and the Word of God.

    Love Community

    We encourage fellowship, prayer, and spiritual growth — building a family rooted in Christ.

    Love People

    Just as Jesus loved unconditionally, we aim to extend that love through the messages we share and the lives we touch.

    What We Do?

    At Perfect Bible Study, we are committed to spreading the light of God's Word through simple, clear, and heartfelt teachings. Our focus is on the life, parables, and promises of Jesus Christ — especially bringing His truth to Kannada-speaking audiences. Through videos, blogs, and reflections, we aim to guide every viewer into deeper faith and understanding.

    • Teach the Parables of Jesus
    • Share Biblical Wisdom in Kannada
    • Offer Comfort and Hope
    • Build Faith Through Stories
    • Promote Moral Living
    • Create a Space for Reflection
